Chocolate Chip Cookies

This chocolate chip cookie recipe makes delicious cookies with crisp edges and chewy middles


This chocolate chip cookie recipe is truly the best. Just take it from the 14,000 members of the Allrecipes community who have given it rave reviews!

These chocolate chip cookies are beloved because they're soft, chewy, and absolutely irresistible. Our top-rated recipe for chocolate chip cookies will quickly become your go-to.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Baking Soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F

  2. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.

  3. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.

  4. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips

  5. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.

  6. Bake until the edges are golden brown, about 10 minutes.
